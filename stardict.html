lo lojbo vlaste pe la stardikt zi'e noi mi finti ke'a fo la jbovlaste

  • Linux shell script to convert from jbovlaste xml to tab-delimited and LF(\n) text.
  • I picked up languages that contain several dozen of entry words in jbovlaste, and converted the xml dumps into the stardict files.
  • Recommended usage: Download [jbo-jbo] + [your preferable language] + [jbo-eng] + [rafsi-jbo], untar and put them in your dic folder. View them with GoldenDict, Dictionary Universal and so on.
