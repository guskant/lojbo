lo lojbo vlaste pe la stardik zi'e noi mi finti ke'a fo la jbovlaste

  • Linux shell script to convert from jbovlaste xml to tab-delimited and LF(\n) text.
  • I picked up languages that contain at least several dozen of entry words in jbovlaste, and converted the xml dumps into the stardict files.
  • Recommended usage: Download [jbo-jbo] + [your preferable language] + [jbo-eng] + [rafsi-jbo] + [selmaho-jbo], untar and put them in your dic folder. View them with a dictionary application for StarDict.
  • Some dictionary applications for StarDict format (though I cannot verify the functions except Linux):
    • Linux: GoldenDict, StarDict
    • Mac: Mac Dictionary Kit converting StarDict format to Dictionary 2.0 format, StarDict
    • Windows: GoldenDict, StarDict
    • iPhone, iPod touch, iPad:
      • Free: Taptionary (ki'e la cogas noi facki)
      • Charged: Dictionary Universal, GuruDic, WeDict Pro, Fora Dictionary Pro
    • Android:
      • Free: GoldenDict Free, Word Mate, Fora Dictionary, AntTek Dict
      • Charged: GoldenDict
    • Windows phone:
      • Free: SilverDict, SDict, gTongue
      • Charged: gTongue Pro
  • For those who cannot view plural dictionaries at once, I created single dictionaries that consist of popular combination of dictionaries.
