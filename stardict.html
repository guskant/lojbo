lo jbovlaste pe la stardikt

  • I picked up languages that contain at least several dozen of entry words in jbovlaste, and converted the xml dumps into stardict format.
  • Recommended usage: Download [jbo-jbo] + [your preferable language] + [jbo-eng] + [rafsi-jbo] + [selmaho-jbo], untar and put them in your dic folder. View them with a dictionary application for StarDict. For applications that cannot show plural dictionaries at once, try one of the some-in-one files that are combination of dictionaries.
  • Tips: selma'o entries are prefixed with "+". For example, type "+nu" for looking up NU entries.
  • Convert by yourself? You may use my ugly converter (linux shell script) and a patch to stardict-tools-3.0.1.
